What is PiggyBank?

PiggyBank is a revolutionary new way to save up the change you receive from businesses and free up some space in your pocket. When you go to a business and receive change you now have an option. You can either get the change, and put it in your pocket (and possibly lose the change), or you can take out your smartphone and store the change on PiggyBank and see how much the change adds up! When you reach a particular amount you can go back to the place of business and use the change you saved up towards that next transaction. It is a fun, easy, and safe way to add up all of that change you receive from businesses.

Designed with YOU in mind!

We designed PiggyBank with the latest technologies to give our users the best experience they can ask for. With our simple user interface it is quick, secure, and convenient to use PiggyBank

Time saver

PiggyBank was designed from the ground to save you time. Time is valuable. Why spend your time putting change away?
You can easily just put it into PiggyBank and see it add up! It only takes a few seconds and it's easy to use.
